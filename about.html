About Sonsek Cleaning & Fumigation

A professional cleaning and fumigation company in Abuja, dedicated to protecting your home, office, and health with safe and reliable services.

Our story in Abuja

Sonsek Cleaning & Fumigation Services was established to solve a simple problem in Abuja: finding a cleaning and fumigation partner you can trust inside your home, office, and facilities.

From our base at Dunamis/Mummy Road Suite 30, Bello Plaza by Check-point in Nyanya, we support busy families, professionals, businesses, and property managers across Abuja and the FCT. Our teams are trained, supervised, and equipped with quality cleaning agents and safe fumigation products.

Our mission

Our mission is to deliver consistently high-quality cleaning and fumigation that keeps Abuja properties hygienic, comfortable, and pest-free – while treating every client’s space with care and respect.

Our vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted name in professional cleaning and fumigation across Abuja and Nigeria, setting the standard for safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
